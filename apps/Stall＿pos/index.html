Stall POS — Creator Checkout

Offline-first checkout & inventory for creators, makers, and market vendors. No signup, no cloud—everything stays on your device.

What it does

Stall POS is designed for quick, reliable sales at pop-up markets and craft stalls. Large buttons, clear labels, and offline operation help creators focus on selling.

  • Fast checkout: tap items, set quantity, print or show order.
  • Supports cash & record-only electronic payments (no payment gateway).
  • Inventory & product management with images, cost, price, and auto-stock deduction.
  • Gift/promo logic and order summaries (revenue, profit, promo value).
  • All data stored locally — privacy-first by design.

Why Stall POS

Built for makers who need a simple, dependable system at markets. No internet required, easy to learn, and focused on speed.

Quick Specs

  • Offline-first, local storage
  • Tablet-optimized landscape mode
  • Large touch targets and accessible typography
  • Export / backup via local file (user-managed)
