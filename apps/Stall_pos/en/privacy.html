Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 4, 2025

Data Collection and Use

Stall POS is a completely offline POS system. We value your privacy and explain our data handling practices:

  • All data is stored only on your device.
  • We do not collect or upload any transaction data.
  • No account registration is required.
  • We do not track your usage behavior.

Data Security

Your data security is paramount to us:

  • Data is stored only in your device's local storage.
  • It is not uploaded to the cloud or other servers.
  • We recommend backing up important data regularly.

App Permissions

Stall POS requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Storage: Used to store transaction records and product data.
  • Bluetooth: Used to connect to Bluetooth printers (optional feature).
