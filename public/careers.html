Venture Building Model

Don't Get Hired.
Get Equity.

We operate as a Venture Studio, not a traditional employer.
We are looking for Builders who want to own what they create, take calculated risks, and share in the upside of the products we ship.

Skin in the Game.

True partnership requires shared risk and shared reward. We do not believe in unpaid labor, nor do we believe in equity without execution.

Our Philosophy:
We provide the Product Architecture, Design System, and Strategy. You bring the Engineering Prowess or Domain Expertise. Together, we build Minimum Viable Products (MVPs) that evolve into sustainable businesses.

We don't need you to work for us. We need you to build with us.

Who Thrives Here?

The startup journey is non-linear and demanding. We succeed by adhering to these core traits.

Navigating Ambiguity

There are no roadmaps for what we are building. You must be comfortable making decisions with incomplete information and iterating as you go.

Radical Ownership

You don't wait for tasks to be assigned. You identify problems, propose solutions, and own the outcome—whether it's a success or a learning opportunity.

Digital Craftsmanship

We believe code and design are forms of art. We obsess over the details that others ignore, because quality is the only long-term competitive advantage.
