About ELLEZ

ELLEZ LLC, founded by Chou Tsu Yin, Su Kailin, and Su Chuan Tzu, is a multidisciplinary innovation studio focused on the intersection of design, AI, and human experience. We are committed to creating digital products that combine aesthetics and functionality, deeply believing in the values of ethics, creators, and the future of society.

Our mission is to create empowering tools—whether it's a mental health companion, a decentralized commission platform, or a reimagined digital community. We don't just build websites or apps; we create systems that connect ideas to reality.
