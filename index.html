About ELLEZ

Founded by Chou Tsu Yin, Su Kailin, and Su Chuan Tzu, ELLEZ LLC is a cross-disciplinary studio bridging design, AI, and human experience. We specialize in crafting meaningful digital products that combine beauty and functionality, with a deep respect for ethics, creators, and future society.

Our mission is to create tools that empower — whether it's a mental health companion, a decentralized commission platform, or a reimagined digital social space. We don't just build websites or apps. We build systems that connect ideas to reality.

Our Principles

01

Design with Purpose

We don't just build features. We craft meaningful interactions that solve real human problems with elegance and simplicity.
